Werder Bremen

SV Werder Bremen: Sportverein Werder Bremen von 1899 e. V., commonly known as Werder Bremen, is a German sports club based in Bremen. The club's football team has had a long and storied history in the Bundesliga, being one of the founding members and participating in 60 campaigns. Werder Bremen has won the Bundesliga on four occasions and been runners-up seven times, placing them behind only Bayern Munich and Borussia Dortmund in this category. They also achieved success in Europe, winning the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup in 1992 and the UEFA Intertoto Cup in 1998
